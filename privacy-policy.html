Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how TimeSlip ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

Key Point: TimeSlip is designed with privacy in mind. Your time tracking data stays on your device by default, and we don't collect personal information unless necessary for app functionality.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Time Tracking Data: Time entries, categories, notes, and activity logs you create within the app
  • Custom Categories: Category names, icons, and settings you configure
  • App Settings: Your preferences and configuration choices

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: iOS version, device model (for app optimization only)
  • App Usage: Feature usage patterns for improving app performance
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash and error logs through Apple's systems

1.3 Information We DON'T Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Photos or camera access
  • Contacts or social media information
  • Web browsing history

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Core Functionality: Enable time tracking, categorization, and analysis features
  • AI Analysis: Provide personalized insights about your time usage patterns
  • App Improvement: Identify and fix bugs, improve performance
  • Cloud Sync (Pro Users): Sync your data across your devices via iCloud

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All your time tracking data is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework. This data includes:

  • Time entries and duration logs
  • Custom categories and settings
  • AI analysis results and insights

3.2 iCloud Sync (Optional)

Pro users can optionally enable iCloud sync to access their data across multiple devices. When enabled:

  • Data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account
  • We cannot access your iCloud data
  • Data remains under your Apple ID's privacy protection
  • You can disable sync at any time in Settings

3.3 AI Processing

AI analysis of your time patterns is performed:

  • On-device when possible for maximum privacy
  • Via secure, anonymized prompts when cloud processing is needed
  • Without storing personal identifiers on our servers

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

4.1 Service Providers

  • Apple App Store: For subscription processing and app distribution
  • AI Services: Anonymized data for generating insights (when cloud processing is used)

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Data Control

You have full control over your data:

  • View: All your data is accessible within the app
  • Export: Pro users can export data in CSV or PDF format
  • Delete: You can delete individual entries or clear all data
  • Sync Control: Enable or disable iCloud sync at any time

5.2 Data Portability

You can export your time tracking data in standard formats and use it with other applications.

6. Children's Privacy

TimeSlip is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through:

  • App Store update notes
  • In-app notifications
  • Updates to this webpage

8. Third-Party Services

8.1 Apple Services

TimeSlip integrates with Apple services:

  • App Store: Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • iCloud: Subject to Apple's iCloud Privacy Policy
  • StoreKit: For subscription management

8.2 No Analytics Tracking

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@timeslipapp.com

App Store: Leave a review or contact through the App Store

Website: Visit our support page for more information

This Privacy Policy is effective as of January 2025 and applies to all users of TimeSlip.
