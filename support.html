3. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I upgrade to TimeSlip Pro?
A: Tap the "PRO" button in the top-right corner of the app, or go to Settings → Premium. Choose your preferred subscription plan and complete the purchase through the App Store.
Q: Can I cancel my Pro subscription?
A: Yes! Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your device, find TimeSlip Pro, and tap Cancel. You'll retain Pro features until the end of your billing period.
Q: How does iCloud sync work?
A: iCloud sync (Pro feature) automatically backs up your time tracking data to your personal iCloud account. Enable it in Settings → iCloud Sync. Your data syncs across all your devices signed in with the same Apple ID.
Q: Why can I only see 7 days of history?
A: Free users have access to 7 days of time tracking history. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited history and access to all your past entries.
Q: How do I export my data?
A: Pro users can export data by going to Settings → Data Export. Choose CSV for spreadsheet format or PDF for formatted reports. Select your date range and categories to include.
Q: Can I customize categories?
A: Yes! Go to Settings → Manage Categories to create, edit, or delete custom categories. Choose icons, colors, and set category types (Core, Neutral, or Low-Value).
Q: How accurate are the AI insights?
A: AI insights are based on your actual time tracking data and become more accurate with more usage. They provide general productivity guidance but should be considered alongside your personal context and goals.
Q: Does TimeSlip work offline?
A: Yes! Core time tracking functions work completely offline. AI analysis and iCloud sync require an internet connection, but your local data is always accessible.

4. Common Issues and Solutions

4.1 Timer Issues

Timer stops unexpectedly:

  • Check if Background App Refresh is enabled for TimeSlip
  • Ensure Low Power Mode isn't preventing background activity
  • Keep the app active or return to it periodically

Timer doesn't start:

  • Select a category before starting the timer
  • Check that no other timer is already running
  • Restart the app if the issue persists

4.2 Sync Problems

iCloud sync not working:

  • Verify you're signed in to iCloud on all devices
  • Check that iCloud Drive is enabled in device settings
  • Ensure adequate iCloud storage space
  • Try turning sync off and on again in TimeSlip settings

Data not appearing on other devices:

  • Wait a few minutes for sync to complete
  • Check internet connectivity on both devices
  • Force-close and reopen TimeSlip on both devices

4.3 Subscription Issues

Pro features not unlocked after purchase:

  • Restart the app after completing purchase
  • Go to Settings → Restore Purchases
  • Check that payment was successful in your Apple ID account
  • Contact Apple Support for billing issues

Can't access Pro features:

  • Verify your subscription is still active in iOS Settings → Subscriptions
  • Check that you're signed in with the correct Apple ID
  • Restore purchases if switching devices
