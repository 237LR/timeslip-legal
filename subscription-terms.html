3. Free Trial

New subscribers receive a 7-day free trial of TimeSlip Pro features:

  • Full access to all Pro features during the trial period
  • No charge during the 7-day trial
  • Cancel anytime during the trial to avoid charges
  • Trial automatically converts to paid subscription unless cancelled
  • One free trial per Apple ID

4. Billing and Payment

4.1 Payment Processing

  • All payments are processed securely by Apple through the App Store
  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID account
  • Prices include applicable taxes where required
  • Payment method is determined by your App Store account settings

4.2 Billing Cycle

  • Weekly: Charged every 7 days
  • Monthly: Charged every calendar month
  • Yearly: Charged every 12 months

4.3 Auto-Renewal

Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period.

5. Cancellation and Refunds

5.1 How to Cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time through:

  • iPhone/iPad: Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > TimeSlip Pro
  • Mac: App Store > Sign In > View Information > Manage Subscriptions
  • Apple TV: Settings > Users and Accounts > [Your Name] > Subscriptions
  • PC: iTunes > Account > View My Account > Manage Subscriptions

5.2 When Cancellation Takes Effect

  • Cancellation prevents future billing but doesn't immediately revoke access
  • You retain Pro features until the end of your current billing period
  • After expiration, your account reverts to the free tier
  • Your data remains safe and accessible

5.3 Refund Policy

Refunds are handled by Apple according to their standard refund policy:

  • Contact Apple Support for refund requests
  • Refunds are typically available for recent purchases
  • Apple determines refund eligibility on a case-by-case basis
  • TimeSlip cannot directly process refunds
